Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Sparrow is designed as a local macOS utility. It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Data Collection

Sparrow does not collect analytics, usage data, personal information, device identifiers, or crash reports.

Network Access

Sparrow does not require network access for its core functionality and does not send data to external servers.

Local Preferences

Sparrow stores your selected mouse button mappings locally on your Mac using standard macOS preferences. These settings stay on your device.

Input Permissions

Sparrow needs permission to observe global mouse button presses so it can detect the buttons you configure for switching Spaces.

Sparrow does not record your clicks, mouse movement history, keyboard input, screen contents, or application activity. Mouse button events are used only to decide whether to trigger a Space switch.

Third Parties

Sparrow does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or external services.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the support contact provided with the app listing.
